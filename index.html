Turtle Species of Alabama

Alabama is home to a rich diversity of turtle species, from the freshwater rivers of the Mobile-Tensaw Delta to the saltwater shores of the Gulf Coast. Many of these incredible reptiles are facing threats to their survival. This project highlights several key species, providing interactive presentations to explore their habitats, behaviors, and the conservation efforts needed to protect them.
